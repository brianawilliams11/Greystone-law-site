**Client outcome:** Board and owner actions documented and repeatable; governance records organized for diligence and disputes.

## Typical scope (baseline)

- Minutes and consents system
- Approval procedures
- Governance calendar
- Records repository architecture

## What this does NOT include

- Litigation or dispute representation (separately engaged)
- Unbounded backdating or reconstruction work beyond defined scope
